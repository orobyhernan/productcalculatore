WAREHOUSE MAYHEM

Welcome to my little project, this web page simulates a warehouse inventory control where you check the price for all the products that you have. Just input the name of a product in the product column (e.g: smartphone, Jordans, saucepan) and a category (e.g: electronics, footwear, utilities, you name it), a price for each and the quantity of products that you have, and input a category to find out the total price of products for that category. The calculator will do the rest for you. Enjoy !!!
